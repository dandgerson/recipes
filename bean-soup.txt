Ingredients:
 - 2 kg chiken cheast meat
 - 2 cloves garlic, chopped
 - 4 cloves onion
 - 4 midlle carrots
 - One 8-ounce can tomato sauce
 - 2 tablespoons chili powder
 - 1 teaspoon ground adjica
 - 1 teaspoon ground hmeli sumeli
 - 4 teaspoon salt
 - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
 - 1 kg can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
 - 3 l of clean water

Directions:
 1. Brown the chiken cheast meat in a large bot and drain off the excess fat.
 2. Add the tomato sauce, chili powder, adjicf, hmeli sumeli, salt, and cayenne.
 3. Stir onion and carrot and add to chiken meat large bot
 3. add in the bot 1 kg kidney beans and stay them on low flame about 3 hours with 3 l of water.
